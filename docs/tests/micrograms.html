The dose of colecalciferol is measured in micrograms (mcg - sometimes written µg) or international units (IU.) An mcg is a tiny amount - 1,000 times smaller than an mg (milligram). Each 1mcg of colecalciferol is the same as 40 IU.

The word microgram is sometimes written with the Greek symbol µ followed by the letter g (µg). A microgram is 1,000 times smaller than a milligram (mg).

The dose can also be written in international units (IU.) Each 1 microgram of colecalciferol is the same as 40 IU.
